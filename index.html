    Why Forests Are Important ?

    Forests play a vital role in maintaining the health of the planet. They provide oxygen, regulate temperatures, and serve as a home for countless species. Without forests, life as we know it wouldn't exist. They are critical for the ecosystem's balance.

    How Forests saves us ?

    Forests also act as carbon sinks, absorbing CO2 and helping to mitigate the effects of climate change. This makes them crucial in the global effort to combat rising temperatures and environmental degradation.
